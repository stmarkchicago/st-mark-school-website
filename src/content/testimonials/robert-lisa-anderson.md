We transferred our children to St. Mark three years ago, and it was the best decision we've made for our family.

Coming from a traditional school setting, we were amazed by the difference in our children's attitudes toward learning. The Montessori approach taught them to be self-directed, curious learners rather than passive recipients of information. The classical curriculum gives them a rich foundation in the great works of literature, history, and thought.

Most importantly, St. Mark has given our children something we couldn't provide at home alone: a community of peers who share their faith. They're surrounded by friends who pray, who think about virtue, who aspire to become more Christ-like. That peer influence is invaluable during these formative years.

The teachers are exceptional—highly educated, deeply faithful, and genuinely invested in each child's growth. They communicate regularly with us and treat us as partners in education. We feel blessed to be part of the St. Mark family.
