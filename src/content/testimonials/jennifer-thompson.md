As a former public school teacher, I had high standards when looking for a school for my own children. St. Mark exceeded every expectation.

The Montessori method's emphasis on hands-on learning and following the child's natural curiosity is brilliantly integrated with a rigorous classical curriculum. My second grader is reading Latin prayers, discussing virtue, and working independently with mathematical concepts I didn't learn until middle school.

But what truly sets St. Mark apart is the intentional faith formation. This isn't a school that simply adds a Bible class to the schedule—Christ is at the center of everything. My children are learning to see God in literature, math, science, and art. They're developing a Christian worldview that will serve them for life.

The community of families here shares our values and commitment to raising children who love God and love learning. We've found our home.
