We discovered St. Mark when searching for a school that would nurture our children's faith while providing excellent academics. The Montessori approach combined with classical education and Orthodox Christian values was exactly what we were praying for.

Our daughter Sofia has blossomed in the Primary classroom. Her confidence and independence have grown tremendously, and she comes home excited to share what she learned about God each day. Lucas thrives in the structured yet creative environment, and his teachers truly know him as an individual.

What impresses us most is how the school lives out its mission daily. From morning prayers to service projects, our children are learning that faith isn't just something you talk about on Sundays—it's a way of life. The teachers don't just instruct; they disciple. They partner with us as parents, and we feel supported in raising our children in the faith.

St. Mark has become our extended family, and we're so grateful God led us here.
