St. Mark Christian School is excited to offer our **Summer Discovery Camp** for students entering Kindergarten through 6th grade!

## Program Details

**Dates:** June 10 - July 26 (7 weeks)
- Week-by-week registration available
- No camp July 4th week

**Hours:** 8:30 AM - 3:00 PM
- Extended care available 7:30 AM - 5:30 PM

## Weekly Themes

Each week features a unique theme combining hands-on learning, arts, science, outdoor exploration, and faith formation:

1. **Creation Celebration** - Exploring God's amazing creation
2. **Around the World** - Discovering cultures and geography
3. **Young Scientists** - Hands-on STEM experiments
4. **Creative Arts** - Drama, music, and visual arts
5. **Great Outdoors** - Nature exploration and environmental stewardship
6. **History Detectives** - Time-traveling through history
7. **Service & Leadership** - Learning to serve like Jesus

## What's Included
- Daily chapel and prayer
- Montessori-inspired activities
- Art projects and music
- Outdoor play and games
- Healthy snacks
- Swimming (select weeks)
- Field trips

## Registration
**Tuition:** $285/week or $1,800 for all 7 weeks (10% discount)

Register online or contact our office at (630) 986-1100. Space is limited!

Make this summer meaningful with learning, fun, and faith!
