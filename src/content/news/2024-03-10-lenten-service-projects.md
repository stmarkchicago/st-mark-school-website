As we journey through the Lenten season, our St. Mark community is embracing opportunities to serve others and grow in faith through action.

## Living Out Our Faith

This Lent, each class has chosen a service project that reflects our commitment to loving God and loving our neighbors:

### Early Childhood (Ages 3-6)
**Project:** Creating care packages for families in need
Our youngest students are learning about compassion by assembling blessing bags with essential items for local families experiencing hardship.

### Lower Elementary (Grades 1-3)
**Project:** Partnering with Feed My Starving Children
Students will pack meals and learn about global hunger while discovering how small acts of service can make a big difference.

### Upper Elementary (Grades 4-6)
**Project:** Nursing home ministry
Our older students will visit local nursing homes, bringing joy through crafts, songs, and conversation with elderly residents who often feel forgotten.

## Prayer and Fasting

In addition to service, our students are learning about the spiritual disciplines of prayer and fasting (age-appropriate):
- Daily prayers for those we serve
- Giving up screen time to spend time reading Scripture
- Sacrificial giving from allowances to support ministries

## Family Participation Welcome

Families are invited to join us in these service opportunities! Contact your child's teacher for specific dates and details.

*"For even the Son of Man came not to be served but to serve, and to give his life as a ransom for many." - Mark 10:45*

Let us embrace this season of preparation with hearts ready to serve!
