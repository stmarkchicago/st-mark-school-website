**Mark your calendars!** Our Spring Open House is scheduled for **Friday, March 15th from 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM**.

## Experience the Montessori Difference

This special event offers prospective families the opportunity to:
- **Tour our prepared environments** and see Montessori materials in action
- **Observe children at work** in our Primary and Elementary classrooms
- **Meet our certified Montessori guides** and ask questions
- **Learn about our classical curriculum integration**
- **Discover how faith is woven throughout the day**

## What Makes St. Mark Unique?

At St. Mark, we believe that Montessori's respect for the child perfectly complements our Orthodox Christian values. Our approach honors each child as an image-bearer of God, created with unique gifts and a natural desire to learn.

### Our Program Highlights:
- AMI/AMS certified Montessori guides
- Multi-age classrooms (3-6, 6-9, 9-12)
- Classical education integration
- Daily chapel and prayer
- Beautiful, prepared environments
- Low student-to-teacher ratios
