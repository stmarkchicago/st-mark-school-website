# Quick Content Editing Guide

## 🎯 How to Update Your Website Content

This guide shows you how to edit news, events, and testimonials on your St. Mark website using the simple admin panel.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the Admin Panel
1. Go to: `https://stmarkchristian.school/admin`
2. You'll see the Decap CMS login screen
3. Click "Login with Netlify Identity" or your configured method

### Step 2: Navigate Collections
Once logged in, you'll see three main collections on the left:
- 📰 **News & Stories**
- 💬 **Testimonials**
- 📅 **Events**

Click any collection to see all items in it.

---

## ✏️ Editing Existing Content

### To Edit a News Article, Event, or Testimonial:

1. **Click the collection** (News, Events, or Testimonials)
2. **Find the item** you want to edit
3. **Click on the item** to open it
4. **Make your changes** in the editor
5. **Click "Save"** (top right)
6. **Click "Publish"** to make it live
7. **Wait 1-2 minutes** for the changes to appear on your site

### What You Can Edit:
- ✅ Title
- ✅ Date
- ✅ Featured status (toggle on/off)
- ✅ Body text/content
- ✅ Images
- ✅ Author name
- ✅ Categories
- ✅ Excerpts

---

## ➕ Creating New Content

### To Add a New News Article:

1. Click **"News & Stories"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"New News & Stories"** button (top right)
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Title**: Your article headline
   - **Date**: When it was written
   - **Author**: Your name or "St. Mark Staff"
   - **Category**: Choose from dropdown (Community, Admissions, etc.)
   - **Featured**: Toggle ON for homepage display
   - **Image**: Click "Choose an image" to upload
   - **Excerpt**: Short 1-2 sentence summary
   - **Body**: Write your full article (use formatting tools at top)
4. Click **"Save"** then **"Publish"**

### To Add a New Event:

1. Click **"Events"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"New Events"** button
3. Fill in:
   - **Title**: Event name
   - **Date**: Event date (YYYY-MM-DD format)
   - **Start Time**: When it begins (e.g., "6:30 PM")
   - **End Time**: When it ends
   - **Location**: Where it takes place
   - **Category**: Type of event
   - **Featured**: Toggle for homepage
   - **Image**: Upload event photo
   - **Registration Link**: URL or just use `/admissions/inquire`
   - **Body**: Full event description
4. Click **"Save"** then **"Publish"**

### To Add a New Testimonial:

1. Click **"Testimonials"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"New Testimonials"** button
3. Fill in:
   - **Name**: Parent/family name
   - **Role**: "Parent of [student names and grades]"
   - **Featured**: Toggle for homepage
   - **Image**: Family photo
   - **Date**: When submitted
   - **Body**: The testimonial text
4. Click **"Save"** then **"Publish"**

## 🖼️ Adding Images

### Method 1: Upload New Image
1. In any content editor, find the **Image** field
2. Click **"Choose an image"**
3. Click **"Upload"** (top right of image selector)
4. Select your image file from computer
5. Click the uploaded image to select it
6. Click **"Choose selected"**

### Method 2: Use Existing Image
1. Click **"Choose an image"**
2. Browse images already uploaded
3. Click the one you want
4. Click **"Choose selected"**

### Image Best Practices:
- **News articles**: 1200 x 800 pixels
- **Events**: 1200 x 800 pixels
- **Testimonials**: 400 x 400 pixels (square)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File size**: Under 1 MB (compress if larger)

---

## 🗑️ Deleting Content

1. Open the item you want to delete
2. Click the **"Delete"** button (top toolbar)
3. Confirm you want to delete it
4. It will be removed from your site immediately

⚠️ **Warning**: Deleting is permanent! There's no undo.

---

## 📝 Formatting Your Content

The editor toolbar has buttons for:
- **Bold** and *Italic* text
- Headings (H2, H3, H4)
- Bullet lists and numbered lists
- Links
- Quotes
- Images in content

### Using Headings:
```
## Section Heading (use H2)
### Subsection (use H3)
#### Small heading (use H4)
```

### Adding Links:
1. Highlight the text you want to link
2. Click the link button (chain icon)
3. Enter the URL
4. Click "OK"

### Adding Scripture Quotes:
Use the italic button or format like this:
*"For God so loved the world..." - John 3:16*

## ✨ Tips for Great Content

### News Articles
- **Start with a hook**: Grab attention in the first sentence
- **Use real names**: Makes it more personal and authentic
- **Include dates**: Specific dates for events or deadlines
- **Add a call-to-action**: Tell readers what to do next
- **End with Scripture**: Reinforce the faith focus

### Events
- **Be specific**: Exact dates, times, locations
- **List what to bring**: Help families prepare
- **Include RSVP info**: How and when to respond
- **Describe what happens**: Give a clear schedule
- **Answer questions**: Address common concerns

### Testimonials
- **Keep it real**: Authentic voices resonate
- **Be specific**: Mention specific teachers, programs, moments
- **Show transformation**: Before and after
- **Include grades**: Helps parents relate
- **Stay positive**: Focus on benefits and blessings

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "My changes aren't showing up"
- Wait 2-3 minutes for the site to rebuild
- Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)
- Check that you clicked "Publish" not just "Save"

### "I can't log in"
- Make sure you're using the correct credentials
- Try clearing cookies and logging in again
- Contact your site administrator

### "The image won't upload"
- Check file size (must be under 10 MB)
- Make sure it's JPG or PNG format
- Try compressing the image first
- Check your internet connection

### "I accidentally deleted something"
- Check your GitHub repository - the file may still be there
- Contact your developer - they may be able to restore it
- In the future, edit instead of delete

## 👥 Giving Others Access

To allow teachers or staff to edit content:

1. They need a Netlify Identity account (or GitHub account)
2. Add them in your Netlify dashboard
3. They can then log in at `/admin`
4. Set appropriate permissions (editor vs. admin)
