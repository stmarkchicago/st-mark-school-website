Our annual Science Fair showcases student curiosity, investigation, and discovery of God's amazing creation!

## Event Details

Students in grades 1-6 will present their scientific investigations, demonstrating:
- The scientific method
- Critical thinking skills
- Creativity and innovation
- Public speaking abilities
- Understanding of God's design in creation

## Schedule
- **5:00 PM** - Doors open, projects on display
- **5:30 PM** - Student presentations begin
- **6:30 PM** - Awards ceremony
- **7:00 PM** - Reception with light refreshments
- **7:30 PM** - Event concludes

## Project Categories
- **Life Sciences** - Biology, botany, zoology
- **Physical Sciences** - Chemistry, physics
- **Earth & Space** - Geology, astronomy, meteorology
- **Engineering & Technology** - Design, robotics, coding
- **Environmental Science** - Ecology, conservation

## Awards
- Category winners (1st, 2nd, 3rd place)
- Best in Show
- Most Creative
- Best Presentation
- People's Choice Award

All participants receive recognition for their efforts!

## Why Science?

At St. Mark, we believe science is a tool for discovering and understanding God's handiwork. As Psalm 19:1 says, *"The heavens declare the glory of God, and the sky above proclaims his handiwork."*

Our students learn that faith and science aren't opposed—they're complementary ways of exploring truth. Through scientific investigation, we grow in wonder at the Creator's wisdom and design.

## Families Welcome!

This is a FREE community event. All families, friends, and community members are invited to attend and celebrate our students' hard work.

Come be inspired by young scientists exploring God's world!
