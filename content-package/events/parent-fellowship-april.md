Join us for our monthly Parent Fellowship—a time to connect, encourage one another, and grow in faith together.

## This Month's Focus
**"Raising Kids in a Digital Age: Biblical Wisdom for Modern Parenting"**

Guest speaker **Dr. Jennifer Williams**, family counselor and author, will share practical strategies for:
- Setting healthy technology boundaries
- Protecting your child's heart and mind
- Teaching digital discernment
- Modeling healthy device use
- Creating tech-free family time

- **6:30 PM** - Dinner & Fellowship (pizza provided)
- **7:00 PM** - Presentation & Discussion
- **8:00 PM** - Q&A and Prayer
- **8:30 PM** - Closing

Childcare is provided! Please RSVP by April 2nd so we can plan accordingly.

## Monthly Rhythm

Parent Fellowship meets the **first Friday of each month** throughout the school year. These gatherings provide:
- Parenting encouragement and resources
- Prayer support
- Relationship building with other families
- Spiritual growth opportunities
- Practical tools for faithful parenting
