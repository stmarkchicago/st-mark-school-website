Join us for our beloved Annual Fall Festival—a day of family fun, fellowship, and thanksgiving for God's abundant blessings!

## Festival Highlights

### For Kids
- Pumpkin patch and hayrides
- Bounce houses and games
- Face painting
- Petting zoo
- Costume parade (modest costumes encouraged)
- Craft stations

### For Everyone
- BBQ lunch and bake sale
- Live music and performances
- Silent auction
- Book fair
- Raffle prizes
- Food trucks

### Spiritual Focus
- **12:00 PM** - Harvest Blessing & Thanksgiving Prayer
- Scripture readings about God's provision
- Gratitude wall for families to share blessings

## Admission & Tickets
- **Admission:** FREE for all
- **Activity tickets:** $1 each or 12 for $10
- **Lunch plates:** $8 adults, $5 children

All proceeds support our scholarship fund, making St. Mark education accessible to more families.

## Volunteer Opportunities
We need volunteers! Sign up to help with setup, games, food service, or cleanup. Contact volunteer@stmarkchristian.school.

## What to Bring
- Lawn chairs or blankets
- Sunscreen and water bottles
- Cash for tickets and food
- Your appetite and great attitude!

Come celebrate autumn and community with us!
