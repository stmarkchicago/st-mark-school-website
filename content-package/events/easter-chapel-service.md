All families are invited to attend our Easter Chapel Service as we celebrate the resurrection of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.

The service will include:
- Scripture readings
- Hymns and worship songs
- A special message for children
- Fellowship time afterward

**Families and friends are welcome!**

Please arrive a few minutes early to find seating. Light refreshments will be served afterward in the fellowship hall.
