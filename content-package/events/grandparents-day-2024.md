We're excited to welcome grandparents and special friends to campus for a morning of celebration, learning, and fellowship!

## Schedule
- **9:00 AM** - Welcome & Coffee in Fellowship Hall
- **9:30 AM** - Classroom Visits & Student Presentations
- **10:30 AM** - Special Program in the Chapel
- **11:00 AM** - Reception & Refreshments

## What to Expect
Grandparents and special friends will:
- Visit their student's classroom and see Montessori materials in action
- Enjoy student performances, including songs and recitations
- Participate in a special Liturgy of Thanksgiving
- Connect with teachers and other families
- Tour our beautiful campus

## RSVP Required
Please RSVP by **April 26th** to help us plan refreshments and seating. Contact the school office at (630) 986-1100 or info@stmarkchristian.school.

We look forward to honoring the special people in our students' lives!
