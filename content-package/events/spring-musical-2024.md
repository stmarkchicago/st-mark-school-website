Join us for our annual Spring Musical as our Elementary students present C.S. Lewis's beloved classic, "The Lion, The Witch, and The Wardrobe."

## Performance Dates
- **Friday, April 12** at 7:00 PM
- **Saturday, April 13** at 2:00 PM & 7:00 PM
- **Sunday, April 14** at 2:00 PM

## Ticket Information
- **Adults:** $12
- **Students/Seniors:** $8
- **Family Pack (up to 6):** $45

Tickets available at the door or reserve in advance by calling (630) 986-1100.

This production features over 40 student performers, showcasing their talents in acting, singing, and stagecraft. It's a wonderful celebration of the arts and a beautiful allegory of Christ's redemption.
