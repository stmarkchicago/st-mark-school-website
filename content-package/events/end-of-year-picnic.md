Join us for our annual End-of-Year Celebration as we give thanks for a wonderful school year and look forward to summer!

## A Day of Celebration

This special day marks the culmination of our school year with:
- Student recognition and awards
- Final chapel service
- Community picnic
- Games and activities
- Memory-making moments

## Schedule

### 11:00 AM - End-of-Year Chapel
Join us in the auditorium for a special liturgy of thanksgiving:
- Student presentations and performances
- Academic and character awards
- Prayers of gratitude
- Blessing for summer

### 12:00 PM - Picnic Lunch
Head to the lawn for a community feast!
- Main dishes provided by the school
- Families bring side dishes or desserts (signup to follow)
- Beverages and ice cream provided

### 12:45 PM - Games & Activities
- Inflatable obstacle course
- Water games and sprinklers
- Field games (tug-of-war, relay races)
- Face painting
- Yearbook signing station

### 2:00 PM - Closing Prayer & Dismissal

## What to Bring
- Lawn chairs or blankets
- Sunscreen and hats
- Side dish or dessert to share (optional but appreciated)
- Change of clothes for water activities
- Cameras to capture memories!

## Special Recognitions

We'll be honoring:
- **Perfect Attendance**
- **Academic Excellence**
- **Character Awards** (one per class)
- **Service Leaders**
- **Graduating 6th Graders** *(brief ceremony)*

## Looking Ahead

This is also a chance to:
- Pick up summer reading lists
- Learn about Summer Discovery Camp
- Get information about the 2024-2025 school year
- Say farewell to families moving on
