We are overjoyed to welcome 15 new families to St. Mark Christian School this January! As we begin the spring semester, our hearts are full of gratitude for God's faithfulness and provision.

## Growing in Faith Together

Each new family brings unique gifts, talents, and perspectives that enrich our community. From our Early Childhood program to Elementary, we're excited to walk alongside these families as we nurture young hearts and minds in Christ.

## Getting Connected

New families are encouraged to:
- Attend our monthly Parent Fellowship (first Friday of each month)
- Join the Prayer Partners program
- Connect with class parents and room coordinators
- Participate in upcoming service opportunities

## A Special Welcome Event

We're hosting a New Family Welcome Reception on **Friday, January 26th at 6:30 PM** in the Fellowship Hall. This is a wonderful opportunity to meet other families, connect with teachers, and learn more about our community.

Light refreshments will be served, and childcare will be provided. We look forward to celebrating with you!
