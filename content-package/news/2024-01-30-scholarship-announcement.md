We are thrilled to announce a significant expansion of our scholarship and financial aid program for the 2024-2025 school year!

## Our Commitment to Accessibility

At St. Mark, we believe that every family seeking a Christ-centered, classical education for their children should have that opportunity—regardless of economic circumstances. Thanks to the generosity of faithful supporters and a new partnership with the **Illinois Tax Credit Scholarship Program**, we can serve more families than ever before.

## What's New for 2024-2025

### Increased Funding
- Over **$200,000** in scholarship funds available
- Average award: **$5,000 per student**
- Up to **60% tuition assistance** for qualifying families

### New Programs
1. **Need-Based Scholarships** - For families demonstrating financial need
2. **Merit Scholarships** - Recognizing academic excellence and character
3. **Sibling Discounts** - 10% off each additional child
4. **First Responder/Military Grants** - 15% discount for serving families
5. **Pastor/Ministry Family Grants** - Special consideration for ministry families

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Complete FACTS Grant & Aid Assessment
Visit our website and click "Apply for Financial Aid." The FACTS application is confidential and secure.

### Step 2: Submit Supporting Documents
- Most recent tax return
- Pay stubs or income verification
- Statement of need (optional)

### Step 3: Apply to St. Mark
All applicants must also complete an admissions application. Scholarship decisions are made after acceptance.

## Important Dates

- **Priority Deadline:** March 1, 2024 (for current families)
- **New Family Deadline:** April 15, 2024
- **Rolling Awards:** Through June 30, 2024 (as funds are available)

## FAQ

**Q: Will applying for aid affect my child's admission decision?**  
A: No. Admission and financial aid decisions are made separately.

**Q: Do I have to reapply each year?**  
A: Yes. Financial circumstances change, so we reassess annually.

**Q: What if I'm between the "can't afford it" and "can easily afford it" categories?**  
A: We encourage all families to apply! Our goal is to make St. Mark possible for as many families as we can serve.

## Our Mission

Christian education shouldn't be a luxury reserved for the wealthy. It's a gift we want to share with families who desire to raise their children in truth, wisdom, and faith.

We firmly believe that God calls families to St. Mark, and we trust Him to provide the resources. Our job is to be good stewards of those resources and faithful administrators of His provision.
